Welcome to the official CDC website.
Clinical Diagnostic Centre has been in existence since 1989. It is approved by Bureau Veritas as meeting the requirements of ISO 9001:2000 both for UK and USA standards. It emulates the protocols of OSHA (Occupational and Safety and Health Act of the United States of America).
CDC offers numerous health services for the individual, group, corporate body or government in the form of health diagnostic and medical assistant services all under one roof.
We have centres in 2 locations across Mumbai City i.e. CDC North Mumbai in Andheri (E) and CDC South Mumbai in Breach Candy as well as a fully equipped MEDIBUS for groups, offering 360 coverage for our customers.
CDC prides itself on its commitment to our customers and is constantly striving to improve our performance and increase customer delight.
CDC maintains high levels of quality, our practices are ethical and transparent, our rates are reasonable and we aim to far exceed all customer expectations.
The following pages will provide you with a comprehensive overview of CDC and if you require any further assistance please contact us at the earliest and we will be happy to serve you to the best of our abilities.
All of us at CDC look forward to being of service to you and assure you of our best attention at all times.
